This tiramisu cake is made with genoise cake layers soaked with espresso and filled with creamy and silky mascarpone cream, then topped with plenty of cocoa powder. This tiramisu layer cake is light and soft and melts in your mouth. This cake is stunning and is really easy to make and is made with simple ingredients. You can make this for a tiramisu lover's birthday as a birthday cake!
